Accelerometer cell

Need:

  • 1 accelerometer cell
  • 4 jumper wires

Setting up the cell:

  1. Place accelerometer cell on the breadboard.
  2. Using the 4 jumper wires, connect the 3 left pins and 1 rightmost pins from the breadboard to the correct ports of the Raspberry Pi.
    [insert wiring diagram!]

Running the program:

  1. Click the folder icon on the top left of the computer screen.
  2. Select the "accel_test.py" program
  3. Click the play button on the top left of the computer screen.
  4. Try tilting the Raspberry Pi around. You will see the 0 move around on the screen.
