Balancing Dot

Setup:

Need:

  • 1 LED matrix cell
  • 1 accelometer cell
  • 6 male to female jumper wires
  • 5 male to male jumper wire

Steps:

  1. Place the LED matrix cell on the breadboard, using the 4 pins of each matrix as support.
  2. Using the 6 male to female jumper wires, connect the 6 pins of the first matrix (the matrix with the pins sticking out) to the correct ports of the Raspberry Pi.
  3. Using the male to male jumper wire, connect the third left most socket of the last matrix to the 9/MISO port on the Raspberry Pi.
  4. Place accelerometer cell on the breadboard.
  5. Using 4 male to male jumper wires, connect the 3 left pins and 1 rightmost pins from the accelerometer to the correct ports on the Raspberry Pi.
